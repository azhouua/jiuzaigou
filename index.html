景区介绍

Introduction

高原秘境，水与森林的童话

Plateau wonderland of water and forests

九寨沟位于四川省阿坝藏族羌族自治州，以翠海、叠瀑、彩林、雪峰、蓝冰和藏情而闻名，被誉为“人间童话世界”。

Jiuzhaigou, located in Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture of Sichuan, is famed for its emerald lakes, layered waterfalls, colorful forests, snow-capped peaks, blue ice, and Tibetan culture, earning the name "Fairyland on Earth".

景区由树正、日则、则查洼三大沟组成，108个海子串联成高山湖泊群，湖水因光线与矿物质呈现碧蓝、翠绿、琥珀等多变色彩。

The park comprises the Shuzheng, Rize, and Zechawa valleys, where 108 alpine lakes connect into a chain of emerald pools. Minerals and light interplay to create shifting hues of turquoise, jade, and amber across the waters.

碧海叠瀑

Lakes & Waterfalls

108个海子串联，湖水呈现多层次蓝绿，珍珠滩、诺日朗等瀑布群壮观磅礴。

108 alpine lakes glow in layered blues and greens, with iconic waterfalls like Pearl Shoal and Nuorilang.

悠久历史

Long History

形成于数百万年前的高山冰川与喀斯特作用，是川西高原最瑰丽的自然景观之一。

Shaped by alpine glaciers and karst over millions of years, it is one of the most magnificent natural landscapes on the western Sichuan plateau.

雪山森林

Peaks & Forests

雪峰环抱、冷杉与彩林交织，四季呈现冰蓝、翠绿、金黄、银装的层次变化。

Snow peaks cradle fir forests and colorful woods, shifting hues across the four seasons.

藏羌文化

Tibetan & Qiang Culture

藏羌村寨、经幡与特色民俗点缀山谷，生态与文化保护并重。

Tibetan and Qiang villages, prayer flags, and local customs enrich the valleys with culture and conservation.

景区详细介绍

Detailed Introduction

地质形成

Geological Formation

九寨沟是典型的高原喀斯特地貌，冰川作用、地壳抬升与钙华沉积共同塑造了湖泊、瀑布与钙华台地，水体富含碳酸钙形成奶蓝与翡翠色的层次。

Jiuzhaigou is a plateau karst landscape shaped by glaciation, tectonic uplift, and travertine deposition. Calcium-rich waters create tiered tufa terraces, lakes, and waterfalls that glow in milky blue and emerald hues.

主要景点

Main Attractions

主要景点包括五花海、镜海、珍珠滩瀑布、诺日朗瀑布等，每一处都展现出不同层次的水色与彩林倒影。

Highlights include Five Flower Lake, Mirror Lake, Pearl Shoal Waterfall, and Nuorilang Waterfall, each showcasing layered water colors and mirrored forests.

核心景点

Core Attractions

五花海

Five Flower Lake

九寨沟最著名的海子之一，因湖底钙华沉积和藻类分布，呈现出蓝、绿、黄、橙等多种颜色，被誉为"九寨精华"。

One of Jiuzhaigou's most famous lakes, displaying blue, green, yellow, and orange hues due to travertine deposits and algae, known as the "Essence of Jiuzhaigou".

镜海

Mirror Lake

湖面平静如镜，倒映着周围的山峰和彩林，形成完美的对称美景，是摄影爱好者的天堂。

The calm lake surface mirrors surrounding peaks and colorful forests, creating perfect symmetry, a paradise for photographers.

珍珠滩瀑布

Pearl Shoal Waterfall

宽达310米的钙华瀑布，水流从钙华台地倾泻而下，如珍珠般的水珠飞溅，气势磅礴。

A 310-meter-wide travertine waterfall cascading from tufa terraces, with pearl-like droplets creating a magnificent spectacle.

诺日朗瀑布

Nuorilang Waterfall

九寨沟最宽的瀑布，宽270米，高24.5米，藏语意为"雄伟壮观"，是中国最宽的钙华瀑布。

Jiuzhaigou's widest waterfall at 270 meters wide and 24.5 meters high, meaning "magnificent" in Tibetan, China's widest travertine waterfall.

长海

Long Lake

九寨沟最长、最深的海子，海拔3060米，长约7.5公里，最深处达103米，湖水终年碧蓝清澈。

Jiuzhaigou's longest and deepest lake at 3060m elevation, 7.5km long, 103m deep, with crystal-clear blue water year-round.

树正群海

Shuzheng Lakes

由19个大小不等的海子组成的梯级湖泊群，湖水从上游逐级流向下游，形成独特的梯级瀑布群。

A cascade of 19 lakes of varying sizes, with water flowing from upstream to downstream, forming unique tiered waterfalls.

  • High altitude: big temp swings; pack layers and sunscreen
  • 全程观光车+木栈道，注意防滑，遵守单行指引
  • Use shuttles and boardwalks; mind one-way rules and traction
  • 景区实行限流与实名预约，请提前购票
  • Real-name booking and capacity control in effect
  • 请勿投喂野生动物，垃圾随身带走，爱护水体
  • Do not feed wildlife; pack out trash; protect the waters
